This tool will help you estimate the total amount of radiation your protocol will administer to patients.

  • Click + Add Exam for the appropriate exam types in your protocol. Then add the protocol-specific data for each exam.
    The calculator will show a running total per exam type as you add exams.
  • At the bottom of the page, you may add supplemental consent language for your protocol.
  • When you have added all your exams, click 'Generate Report' to prepare your report for the Radiation Safety Committee.
